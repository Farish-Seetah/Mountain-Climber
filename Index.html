Mountain Climber

Venture through a haunting 2D world where every step may mean death in the Mountain. Fight ancient foes, uncover hidden paths, and forge your destiny in a pixelated realm where courage meets shadow.

About the Game

Mountain Climber is a pixel art 2D platformer where atmosphere and challenge intertwine. Explore cursed lands, uncover lore, and face off against creatures drawn from the depths of forgotten worlds. Each level offers unique layout, hidden secrets, and immersive atmosphere.

⚔️ Combat

Engage enemies using skill-based combat inspired by classic side-scrollers.

🎮 How to Play

W – Jump
A – Move Left
D – Move Right

🎵 Atmosphere

Lose yourself in haunting music and a world full of mystery and emotion.
